## Writing a New Process

Use the SOP template or include these sections:

1. **When to use** — what triggers this process
2. **Prerequisites** — access, tools, or permissions needed
3. **Steps** — numbered, specific, copy-pasteable where possible
4. **Verification** — how to confirm it worked
5. **Rollback** — how to undo if something goes wrong
6. **Owner** — who to ask if the process is unclear or broken
