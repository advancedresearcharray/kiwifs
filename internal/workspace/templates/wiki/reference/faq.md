# FAQ

Frequently asked questions. Add new ones as they come up.

## Wiki

**Q: Where do I put a new page?**
A: Pick the section that matches the content type: `processes/` for
SOPs, `decisions/` for ADRs, `reference/` for glossary-type content.
If unsure, `processes/` is a safe default. See [[welcome]].

**Q: How do I link to another page?**
A: Use wiki links: `[[page-name]]` or `[[folder/page|Display Text]]`.

**Q: Who reviews wiki content?**
A: The page `owner` (listed in frontmatter) is responsible for keeping
it current. Anyone can propose edits.
